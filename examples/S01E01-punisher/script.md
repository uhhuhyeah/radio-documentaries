## [01] SPOKEN · intro
Some records you admire from a distance. A few you climb inside and live in for a
while. Punisher, for me, is firmly the second kind. Tonight, over the next half hour
or so, I want to take you into how it was made... where it came from, who was in the
room, and why it lands the way it does. Stay with me.

## [02] SPOKEN · part-1
She wrote most of it in the gaps of other people's tours. Hotel rooms, tour vans,
the strange dead hours between a soundcheck and a stage. And you can hear that in the
bones of it... a low, insomniac hum running under even the prettiest moments. This
first one we'll play grew out of exactly that restlessness.
