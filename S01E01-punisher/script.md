The album was recorded at the legendary Sound City Studios in Van Nuys, California, a space steeped in musical history, famous for its groundbreaking recordings from greats like Fleetwood Mac and Nirvana. Bridgers praised the atmosphere of Sound City, attributing part of its magic to the presence of long-time producer Tony Berg, who has worked on her projects from the start. Recording over a year and a half allowed Bridgers to shape her vision with meticulous attention to detail, harnessing a collaborative spirit that produced stunning tracks filled with emotional honesty. 

Collaborative songwriting was vital to the album's formation. Bridgers worked with Conor Oberst on “I Know the End,” blending their lyrical talents into a powerful narrative. This partnership, along with her work with Christian Lee Hutson on “Halloween,” further illustrates how *Punisher* is filled with shared experiences and reflections on life’s complexities.
